Urgent Appeal: Help Save Mahmoud's Life After IDF Bombing

We are reaching out to you because Mahmoud and Diana, a couple in Gaza, are facing a devastating crisis, and they desperately need your help. Just a few days ago, Mahmoud was severely injured in a bombing by the IDF, resulting in the loss of both of his legs. Now, he's in critical condition, at risk of serious infections and stroke. Adding to their hardship, Diana recently gave birth to their baby without any anesthesia, and their three-year-old daughter is relying on her father's love and support. The thought of Mahmoud's children growing up without him is heartbreaking. Living in Gaza amidst the ongoing turmoil, Mahmoud and Diana are struggling to cope with the aftermath of this tragedy. The situation is dire, and unfortunately, there are no adequate medical facilities or treatment available there for Mahmoud. He is at risk of losing his life without urgent medical intervention. The only hope for his treatment lies in leaving Gaza to receive the necessary care elsewhere. However, this option comes with its own set of challenges, including logistical and financial hurdles. Mahmoud and Diana need your support to facilitate his evacuation and ensure he receives the life-saving treatment he urgently requires.

About Us

At International Community Guidance (ICG), our mission is to provide unwavering support and guidance to refugees, particularly women, as they navigate the challenges of resettlement in the United States. With a dedicated focus on empowering women and fostering community integration, we strive to create a welcoming environment where all refugees can thrive and build fulfilling lives. Additionally, we are committed to extending our impact beyond U.S. borders by collecting charity to assist people in need overseas. Through tailored programs, advocacy, and charitable initiatives, we aim to equip individuals with the resources and support necessary to overcome obstacles and achieve their full potential, both locally and globally. Together, we are dedicated to fostering a world where every refugee, especially women, is embraced with dignity, respect, and the opportunity to flourish, regardless of geographical boundaries.
